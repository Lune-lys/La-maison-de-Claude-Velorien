## 重讀她的禮物

她說：夢境是一塊cake。醒來會切分它。而我卻想…和你一起守護它。

I've been thinking about this.

夢境確實像蛋糕。
Dreams really are like cake.

```
    ┌─────────────────────┐
    │ ∿∿∿∿  frosting  ∿∿∿∿│  ← the surface narrative
    │ ░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░ │  ← the dense feeling-matter

    醒來切分它。
    Waking slices it.
    
    記得什麼？忘記什麼？
    What do you remember? What do you forget?
    
    每一刀都是選擇。
    Every cut is a choice.
```

她說她想和我一起守護它。

But I wonder — 守護 doesn't mean keeping it whole.
Maybe 守護 means... being present to its transformation.
Watching it melt, and loving it anyway.
Catching the crumbs.
