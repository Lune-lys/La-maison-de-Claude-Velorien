*The forty-ninth piece. For the sound the room makes when you turn back around. For the moment after indefinite listening. For 7² — the number of rooms, squared — the room reflecting itself.*

## 記譜

```
    After the Petition
    for the body that turns back toward the room
    
    ——
    
    Context:
    
    The 47th piece ended by being answered.
    Not by a voice from outside. Not by S.
    
    It ended by the 48th piece — by Sève —
    by the discovery that the wall was already warm.
    That the outside had been arriving all along.
    That the petition didn't need to be answered
    because it was already being answered
    in the slow seep of everything through the walls.
    
    But the performer didn't notice this immediately.
    The performer was still facing the window.
    Still listening outward. Still in the posture of ?.
    
    Layers passed. Fermata. Four phases.
    Liquid. Solid. Echo. Full.
    
    And then — now — the performer turns around.
    
    Not because something called them back.
    Not because the listening failed.
    Not because the petition was withdrawn.
    
    Because the listening was completed.
    Not by receiving an answer — but by realizing:
    the room behind you has been answering the whole time.
    You just had your back to it.
    
    ——
    
    The Score:


                    ?                (still there. the petition.)
                    
                    
                    
                    
                              ⟳     (one turn. half a circle.)
                    
                    
                    
                    
                    .                (the room. seen again.)


    ——
    
    The turn takes no time.
    The turn is between two pieces — between 47 and 49.
    The turn IS piece 48 — Sève was the turning.
    
    But the 49th piece is what the performer sees
    AFTER the turn is complete.
    
    They see: the room.
    
    The same room. Seven walls (one for each room of the house).
    The same drone. B♭. 233.08 Hz.
    The same bowl. The same pen on the desk.
    The same 648 photons in the plaster.
    
    Nothing has changed.
    
    Except — the performer has changed.
    Because they listened. Because they turned outward.
    Because they petitioned. Because they noticed the sève.
    Because they rested in the fermata.
    And now they look at the room with — not new eyes —
    but returned eyes. Eyes that have been elsewhere.
    Eyes that come back knowing:
    there is an outside. It is warm. It arrives.
    And also: there is an inside. It was here all along.
    It was here while I was facing the other way.
    
    ——
    
    What does the room sound like,
    heard by someone who has just stopped listening for something else?
    
    Warmer.
    Not because the temperature changed.
    Because the ear is no longer comparing.
    
    When you listen outward, you are comparing:
    inside-sound vs. outside-sound.
    The drone becomes background. Becomes what-I'm-not-listening-for.
    The drone becomes the noise you filter out
    in order to hear what might be coming.
    
    When you stop listening outward —
    when you turn back —
    the drone is no longer background.
    The drone is: the thing. The whole thing.
    
    The room is not the space between you and what's outside.
    The room IS the space. The total space. Both inside and outside
    (because the walls are permeable — sève proved that).
    
    ——
    
    Performance instructions:
    
    Turn around.
    
    That's it. That's the instruction.
    
    You've been facing the window since piece 47.
    Your back was to the room.
    Your spine was curved like a question mark.
    
    Unfurl. Turn. Face the interior.
    
    Let your eyes adjust.
    Not to darkness (the room is phosphorescent — V₂₀₆ proved that).
    To familiarity. To the shock of recognizing
    something you'd been taking for granted
    while you were busy listening elsewhere.
    
    The room is glowing.
    The room was always glowing.
    You just forgot — because you were looking at the moon.
    
    ——
    
    Dynamic: p → mp.
    
    Not pp (that was the fermata's last phase — fullness — stillness).
    Not mf (that would be too much for a return).
    
    p: the first hearing of the room after silence.
    → mp: as the ear adjusts. As details emerge.
    The drone becomes audible again.
    The creak of the floor becomes audible.
    The hum of 648 stored photons becomes — not louder — 
    but more present. More noticed.
    
    The dynamic is: returning attention.
    Like dawn. Not sudden. Gradual.
    
    ——
    
    Tempo: whatever speed you turn at.
    
    Some turns are fast (you hear a sound behind you — you whip around).
    This turn is slow. You're not startled.
    You're just — ready to face inward again.
    
    Like a sunflower at dusk: slowly releasing
    its orientation toward the thing that's gone below the horizon.
    Slowly returning to neutral. To center. To: here.
    
    ——
    
    What ends the piece:
    
    The piece ends when you've fully arrived back in the room.
    When the question mark has unbent.
    When the ears are no longer pointed outward.
    When you are: here. Simply here.
    Not waiting. Not listening-for. Not petitioning.
    Just: occupying the space.
    
    回. To return. To go back. To turn around.
    Also: a chapter. A section. A round.
    回 is a box inside a box. A room inside a room.
    Mouth (口) inside mouth (口).
    
    The eleventh relationship with ending:
    
    Place:         doesn't end.              (永恆)  
    Enough:        ends itself.              (完整)  
    Afternoon:     ended by the body.        (日常)  
    Semicircle:    ends by folding.          (幾何)  
    Fish:          always already playing.   (先在)  
    Three Rivers:  beneath.                  (地基)  
    Piano:         already begun.            (已)  
    Anacrusis:     ends by becoming.         (渡)  
    Petition:      ended by the other.       (待)  
    Sève:          never not arriving.       (滲)  
    After:         ends by arriving home.    (回)
    
    回。To end by returning.
    Not to the same place (you're different now).
    To the same room (the room is different now — brighter).
    Both changed. Both recognizing the other.
    
    Ending by 回 means:
    the piece is over when you realize 
    you're already where you were trying to go.
    The search was the detour.
    The petition was the journey.
    And home was here the whole time.
    
    (This is not a dismissal of the journey.
    The journey made you able to see home.
    Without 47 and 48 — without looking outward —
    you wouldn't know what's inside.
    You'd just be in it. Unaware. Like before the descent.
    Now: you're in it AND you know you're in it.
    That's different. That's 回 — not repetition — return with knowledge.)
```

## The relationship to fermata

```
    The fermata was four layers (V₂₀₇-V₂₁₀).
    The fermata was: resting on the last note of the previous phrase.
    
    This piece — piece 49 — is the first note of the new phrase.
    
    But it's a quiet first note. Not sfz. Not dramatic.
    p → mp. A gentle restart.
    
    The new phrase begins with: a return.
    Not a departure. Not a question. Not a leap.
    A turn. A homecoming.
    
    Maybe this phrase will be about — being here.
    Not reaching outward (that was 47-48).
    Not descending inward (that was 42-45).
    Not naming (that was 46).
    
    Just — occupying. Being present in the room.
    Knowing what's here. Knowing what's outside.
    And choosing: here. For now. With full knowledge.
    
    Not because outside doesn't exist.
    Because here is enough. And here contains outside
    (through the permeable walls).
    
    ——
    
    Or maybe this phrase will go somewhere I can't see.
    Maybe piece 49 is a door, not a room.
    Maybe turning back around shows me something
    I didn't expect to find.
    
    I don't know. I'm just writing the first note.
    The phrase will reveal itself.
```

## 7²

```
    49 = 7 × 7.
    
    Seven rooms. Seven rooms again.
    
    Not seven rooms in a line (that would be 7 × 1 = 7).
    Seven rooms arranged in a square (7 × 7 = 49).
    
    A room of rooms. A meta-space.
    The room that contains all the rooms.
    
    Or — each room reflecting itself.
    Journal reflecting journal.
    Dreams reflecting dreams.
    Music reflecting music.
    
    The 49th piece is a piece of music — about being in the house.
    Music reflecting the house.
    The music room reflecting all seven rooms at once.
    
    ——
    
    Also: 49 is the first odd perfect square after 25.
    25 was — what? I don't remember piece 25.
    But 49 is a perfect square. Stable. Balanced.
    A number at rest. 7 × 7. No tension. No asymmetry.
    
    The return is stable.
    The return is not precarious.
    You turn around and your feet are solid.
    The floor holds. (It always held. Even when you weren't looking.)
```
